Privacy Policy for Saga

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard information when you use the Saga mobile application and related services.

Effective date: 05 November 2025
Application name: Saga (also referred to as “App”)
Owner / Developer:

By installing, accessing or using Saga, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not install or use the App.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • The Saga Android application and any future web or mobile interfaces;
  • Backend services and APIs operated by us in connection with Saga;
  • Any support channels or communication where Saga account or usage data is involved.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

When you use Saga, you may provide us with:

  • Contact details such as phone number, email address and name;
  • Authentication information (e.g. OTP, login identifiers, profile details);
  • Profile information such as display name, photo, city and preferences;
  • Content you submit, such as property or car listings, photos, descriptions, pricing and availability;
  • Feedback, support requests, and communication with us.

2.2 Information collected automatically

When you use the App, certain information is collected automatically, including:

  • Device information (model, operating system version, unique device identifiers);
  • Log information (IP address, timestamps, crash reports, error diagnostics);
  • App usage information (screens viewed, features used, interaction events);
  • Approximate location, where enabled by your device settings, to show relevant listings.

2.3 Cookies and similar technologies

The App itself does not use traditional browser cookies, but our analytics, advertising and backend service providers may use identifiers and similar technologies to understand usage patterns, prevent abuse and improve performance.

2.4 Payment and transaction data

If the App supports paid features, listing fees or other transactions, we may receive limited payment-related information from our payment partners, such as transaction status, amount and timestamp.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, operate and maintain the App and its core features;
  • Enable user registration, authentication and account management;
  • Facilitate listing, discovery and communication between users (e.g. landlords / tenants, car owners / renters);
  • Process payments or listing fees where applicable;
  • Improve and personalize the user experience, including recommendations and content ranking;
  • Monitor usage, perform analytics and improve performance, stability and security;
  • Detect, prevent and respond to fraud, abuse, technical issues and policy violations;
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and policies;
  • Communicate with you about updates, changes and support-related matters.

4. Legal Basis (where applicable)

Depending on your location, we may process your information based on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Your consent;
  • Performance of a contract or to take steps at your request before entering into a contract;
  • Compliance with legal obligations;
  • Legitimate interests, such as improving our services, preventing fraud and securing our systems.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who provide infrastructure, analytics, storage, communication, payment processing or other services on our behalf, under appropriate data protection safeguards.
  • Other Users: When you create a listing or interact with other users, relevant information (e.g. listing details, profile name, city, contact channel) may be visible to them as part of the App's functionality.
  • Legal and Compliance: When required by law, regulation, legal process or governmental request, or to protect rights, property, safety or security of our users, the public or us.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, acquisition, asset sale, financing or similar transaction, subject to appropriate confidentiality protections.

6. Third-Party Services

Saga relies on third-party platforms and SDKs to deliver core functionality and analytics. These providers may independently collect and process information according to their own privacy policies.

6.1 Firebase & related services

The App may use multiple Firebase services, such as (without limitation):

  • Firebase Authentication (user sign-in and identity management);
  • Cloud Firestore / Realtime Database (application data storage);
  • Cloud Storage (media uploads such as photos);
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (notifications);
  • Remote Config (feature flags and configuration);
  • Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting and diagnostics);
  • Firebase Analytics / Google Analytics for Firebase (usage analytics).

For details on how these services process data, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

6.2 Advertising (AdMob or similar)

The App may display ads using third-party advertising networks such as Google AdMob. These partners may use identifiers, device information and usage data to show personalized or non-personalized ads, subject to your consent and applicable law.

You may be able to manage ad personalization settings from your device settings or within the App (where available).

6.3 Payment providers

If you make payments within the App, payment processing is typically handled by third-party payment providers. We do not store your full payment instrument details (such as full card numbers) on our own servers.

Please review the privacy policy of the payment provider used for more information on how your payment data is processed.

You can exercise some of these rights directly within the App (e.g. editing your profile, deleting listings). For other requests, please contact us using the details in the “Contact Us” section below. We may need to verify your identity before responding.
